// The problem

Three Ways AI Breaks Down

Pattern 01
Drift

The AI starts solving problems outside your scope. It modifies files you never mentioned. It changes logic without declaring intent. Each unchecked action compounds the risk and the cost.

Pattern 02
Hallucination

The AI generates confident, plausible-sounding answers that are simply wrong. Code that looks correct but breaks. Documentation that misleads. Decisions built on fiction — billed as fact.

Pattern 03
Data Exposure

Without guardrails, AI assistants handle API keys, credentials, and sensitive data in ways you never approved. Not edge cases — default behaviour of an unstructured session.

We gave AI assistants the power of a senior engineer — and none of the process controls we expect from one. The capability is there. The process is not. And process is what keeps capability from becoming liability.

// The solution

Three Stages. Every Session.

The AAO Methodology structures every AI assistance session into three stages. The consistency is not a constraint — it is the source of the reliability.

01
Start

The AI acknowledges your governing documents and confirms it will follow all rules. For any task touching three or more files, it produces a complete plan for your review before a single file is touched. You approve or redirect. Nothing moves without your gate.

02
Audit

You stay present. You monitor for drift. Five situations trigger an audit check. One command — /methodology-check — runs a nine-question self-audit on demand. Drift surfaces before it becomes damage.

03
Close

A session is not finished when the work is done. It is finished when it is properly closed. One command triggers the full checklist: file change list, session log, tests confirmed passing, and your seven-point sign-off. Not before.

// Built on proven foundations

Total Quality Improvement

The methodology is grounded in four Total Quality Improvement principles applied directly to AI assistance sessions.

1
Prevention, not correction

Problems are caught before they become damage — through planning, review, and confirmation upstream. Not audited after the fact.

2
Continuous monitoring

Quality is maintained throughout the session, not checked at the end. The audit stage runs continuously — not as a post-mortem.

3
Human orchestration

The human stays in control. AI recommends and executes. The human approves and closes. Every gate. Every decision point. Every transition.

4
Process discipline

Every session follows the same structure. Consistency is what makes quality repeatable — and what makes reliable delivery possible.
